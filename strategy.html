Our Strategy

Our strategy is to bring together partners, individuals, and volunteers who are passionate about women empowerment in Nigeria. We understand that sometimes a passionate do-gooder may not have the reach to people who may need empowerment. On the other hand, we understand that there are a lot of deserving women in our communities who without the right exposure may never get empowered. So, in essence; we bring together the givers and receivers ensuring optimal results for the both sides.

Our pioneer partners . . .

The Good Samaritan

The Good Samaritan is our chief financier, providing more than 90% of the finances for our empowerment projects. We are also thankful for their support in times of emergency projects.

Nulchy TV

Nulchy TV, an open-reality TV show operating out of Massachussetes, USA is our chief logistics partner. Nulchy TV ensures that our people get to the places they need to in good time.

Ladybug Web DevOps

Ladybug Web DevOps is our technical expert, providing all the technical services needed for our operation. They ensure that our website and other digital resources stay up-to-date.
